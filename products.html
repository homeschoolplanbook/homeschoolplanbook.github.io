Elementary Edition

Price: $19.99

This book is for the records of one elementary student, and is our most popular book of all. Parents may track in detail the work done for a complete calendar year--seven days per week, fifty-two weeks per year. The weekly pages have nine subjects listed across the top, and days of the week (all seven) listed down the side. Hours may be tallied at the bottom of the page, or on the separate pages near the back. Also included are pages for recording books read, memory work, a grade card, field trips, projects, and more. Designed for grades K-8th

High School Edition

Price: $19.99

Regardless of your student's future plans, this book will neatly keep the necessary records. The weekly-pages format is a two-page spread. The days of the week are listed down the side, with spaces for subjects across the top. These spaces are left blank so that you can fill them in. Designed for grades 9-12

Family Edition

Price: $22.49

This is very similar to the Unit Studies Edition and the Elementary Edition. The only difference is in the weekly pages. Both left and right hand pages are subdivided into two large blocks that are further subdivided into small sections. These small sections are labeled by subject. Work is therefore tracked by the week, not by the day.
